About Us

Luna Pizzeria is a family‑owned restaurant dedicated to serving handcrafted pizzas made with fresh ingredients and baked in our stone oven. Our recipes blend tradition with modern flavors to bring you an unforgettable dining experience—whether you’re dining in, taking out, or ordering for delivery.
